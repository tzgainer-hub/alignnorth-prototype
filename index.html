The People

Trained. Certified. Yours.

Six specialists working under one studio — every session private or duet, every plan built around your body and your history.

01

Rachel

Personal Trainer

NASM-CPT and Corrective Exercise Specialist with twelve years training private clients. Rachel works with members rebuilding strength after injury, surgery, or long breaks from movement — with a specific focus on posture, gait, and the small muscle groups most programs ignore. Former collegiate volleyball player; trains everyone like she's coaching the long game.

02

Carmen

Personal Trainer

NSCA-CSCS and Precision Nutrition Level 1 coach. Carmen builds programs for clients chasing real change in body composition — weight loss, muscle definition, metabolic health — without the boot-camp grind. Bilingual in English and Spanish. Eight years in private studios, including three at a clinical wellness practice in Phoenix.

03

Laura

Personal Trainer

ACE-CPT and Functional Aging Specialist. Laura specializes in clients 55+ who want to keep doing what they love — golf, hiking, tennis, picking up grandchildren — with strong joints and a stable spine. Background in orthopedic rehab gives her a clinician's eye for what's actually safe and what's worth pushing through.

04

Elisa

Physical Therapist

Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) with fourteen years in orthopedic and post-surgical rehab. Elisa works on-site with members recovering from joint replacements, rotator cuff repairs, and chronic back or knee pain — and integrates directly with their training program so recovery isn't a separate appointment. Manual therapy, dry needling, and movement screening certified.

05

Viktoria

Pilates Instructor

Comprehensive Pilates certification through BASI (Body Arts and Science International), with additional training in the Romana's Pilates classical lineage. Viktoria's sessions are known for precise cueing — she sees the form errors most instructors miss. Twelve years teaching reformer, mat, and apparatus. Russian-trained dancer; brings the same attention to alignment to every client.

06

Evgeniya

Pilates Instructor

PMA-Certified Pilates Teacher with comprehensive training through Polestar Pilates. Evgeniya specializes in rehabilitative and pre/post-natal Pilates, with a particular focus on pelvic floor, core, and breath mechanics. Ten years teaching in private studios across two countries. Quiet, methodical, and exceptional at meeting clients exactly where they are.
